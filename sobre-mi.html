About me

I'm methodical, resilient, and passionate about backend development as a way to solve meaningful problems. I'm open to remote opportunities and relocation, especially to Canada or Sweden, where I can grow professionally while supporting my family's future. I believe in sharing knowledge, advocating for junior developers, and building technology that matters.
